# Sentinel Tree

**Objective:** The challenge provided a cryptic four-line riddle, and the goal was to find the exact geographical coordinates of the location it described[cite: 28, 29].

**The Riddle:**
> "In a cloven Sweetwater peninsula, seek the heraldic twin of the winter strawberry capital. [cite: 30]
> Trace a surveyor's track matching a Grand Slam’s opening tally to where a jade beacon hawks hearth-blistered disks. [cite: 31]
> Look across the asphalt to a suburban Pompeii—a silent crater that swallowed the earth whole just as the Advent calendar closed. [cite: 32]
> Rewind the digital lens to a forgotten harvest, and pinpoint where the crimson sentinel's trunk pierced the doomed soil." [cite: 33]

### Step 1: Decoding the Location
I broke down the first line of the riddle. A Google search revealed that the "winter strawberry capital" is Plant City, Florida[cite: 34]. 
The clue then asked for its "heraldic twin." Researching crests, I found that the Fraser clan features a strawberry on its crest[cite: 35]. Fraser also happens to be a city in Michigan, USA[cite: 35]. Checking the map, Fraser is situated near a peninsular region surrounded by the Great Lakes (Sweetwater lakes), confirming I was on the right track[cite: 35].

### Step 2: The Track and the Beacon
Next, I analyzed the second line: "matching a Grand Slam’s opening tally"[cite: 36]. In tennis (a Grand Slam sport), the scoring goes 15, 30, 40, making the opening tally **15**[cite: 38]. 
Because the task was to find coordinates, I scanned the map of Fraser for the number 15 and found **15 Mile Road** at the top of the map[cite: 39, 40].

Along this road, I needed to find a "jade beacon [that] hawks hearth-blistered disks"[cite: 31]. "Jade" means green, and "hearth-blistered disks" is a clever way of describing pizzas. I spotted **Green Lantern Pizza** on 15 Mile Road—a perfect match! [cite: 41]

### Step 3: The Suburban Pompeii
The third line instructed to "Look across the asphalt to a suburban Pompeii—a silent crater..."[cite: 42]. "Across the asphalt" clearly meant looking across the street[cite: 43]. 
The "silent crater" that "swallowed the earth" implied a sinkhole or land subsidence[cite: 44, 45]. The riddle also gave a timeframe: "just as the Advent calendar closed"[cite: 44]. I googled "land sinking in fraser michigan" and discovered news articles about a massive sinkhole that opened up along 15 Mile Road on **December 24-27, 2016** (right around Christmas, the end of the Advent calendar)[cite: 45, 46].

### Step 4: Rewinding the Lens
The final line said to "Rewind the digital lens to a forgotten harvest"[cite: 48]. This meant using Google Earth's historical imagery feature[cite: 44]. 
I looked at the imagery across the road from Green Lantern Pizza between October 2016 and April 2017 to observe the sinkhole site[cite: 46, 47].

The riddle asked to find the "crimson sentinel's trunk"[cite: 48]. A "crimson sentinel" refers to a specific type of tree called a **Crimson Sentry Maple**, which has striking red leaves during the autumn/winter harvest season[cite: 49, 50].
